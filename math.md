## Taylor's formula

$$f(x)=f(x_0)+f'(x_0)\frac{x-x_0}{1!}+f''(x_0)\frac{(x-x_0)^2}{2!}+...+f^{(n)}(x_0)\frac{(x-x_0)^n}{n!}$$

[reference](https://www.zhihu.com/question/21149770): 简单讲就是泰勒展开式与f(x) 每一阶的导数值都相同
